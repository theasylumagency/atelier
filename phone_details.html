The Narrative

Harvested from the flank of the Pata Negra, the 'Pluma' or 'Feather' is a cut of clinical precision and immense rarity. Our interpretation focuses on the architectural structure of the muscle fibers, treated with extreme heat to achieve a glass-like char.

The dish serves as a study in contrast: the heavy, nutty fat of the acorn-fed pork against the sharp, acidic profile of fermented ash-onions.
